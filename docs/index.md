## Introduction

* The purpose of this website is to detail my contributions to Team 303's Sable drone.

### Project Summary

* Team 303's goal for Sable drone is to create a WIFI controlled ground exploration/rescue bot with an arm and grabber attached for sample collection. Sable should also be able to find injured or trapped people in a building and communicate with them.
* To see more information on what our goal is with Sable, please visit my [team report.](https://egr314-s-2026-303.github.io/)

### My Contribution

* My subsystem is the Human Design Interface (HDI). The HDI will be able to control parameters from every other subsytem through a keypad and OLED screen. The OLED screen will also be used to view data being output from the other subsystems. Some examples of data that can be viewed are: Microphone input, Distance from the distance sensor, Camera status, and Wireless status.
